#Project Overview

This Tableau dashboard analyzes restaurant performance metrics from a food delivery marketplace to identify the key drivers influencing customer ratings and overall satisfaction.

The analysis focuses on:
->Pricing impact on ratings
->Delivery time correlation with customer satisfaction
->Cuisine performance comparison
->Top-rated restaurant benchmarking

#Dataset Information

->Total Restaurants: 8,680
->Total Reviews: 1,359,590
->Key Fields: Price, Rating, Delivery Time, Cuisine Type, Popularity Score

#Key Business Questions

->Does higher pricing lead to better ratings?
->How does delivery time impact customer satisfaction?
->Which cuisine types perform best in terms of ratings?
->What characteristics define top-rated restaurants?

#Key Insights

->Delivery speed has a stronger influence on ratings compared to pricing.
->High-priced restaurants do not necessarily receive higher customer ratings.
->Cuisine type plays a significant role in overall performance.
->Marketplace ratings are concentrated within a mid-price band.
